Lubega institute of nursing and medical sciences:

is a registered private training school for nurses and midwives founded in 2016. This school is registered and licensed by the Ministry Of Education Science Technology and Sports and Ministry Of Health. Our agenda is to strengthen the capacity of the National Health System within the principle of the National Health Policy and promote an equitable and efficient improvement in the health status of the population of Uganda and the world at large.

The institute plans started with nursing courses but it’s envisaged that in the next five years, we should be able to initiate and offer other health related and paramedical courses in clinical medicine, laboratory sciences and management courses such as, health education and promotion and health services management among others.Its located in Iganga district along iganga – Malaba highway at buesei village.

Mission;

To provide high quality education in nursing
and other medical sciences for generations
of health professionals with the desired
mix of skills and knowledge to meet national
and global health challenges at an affordable cost.

Vission;

To be the leaders
in producing a multi-skilled competent
and community based health workforce that
will positively impact the trend of
health care in the world through
practical and feasible innovations.
